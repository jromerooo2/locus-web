Preguntas Frecuentes - locus:

Why to Locus Systems?

Because Locus cares about quality in each process, making it a friendly, fast and safe system.

Whom is the system aimed for?

Locus is aimed at companies that travel frequently and need quality software to control them.

Does the system have any cost?

The creation of the system had an educational purpose, so its price is not defined, if you are interested in the system contact us in the corresponding section.

What is the purpose of the system?

The purpose of the system is to facilitate the management of transport units, drivers, profits, costs and maintenance generated by the company.

What password recovery methods does the system have?

The system has: recovery through administrator, through email and recovery through security PIN.

In how many sections is the system and its functionalities divided?

The system is divided into the sections of users, clients, units, maintenance, users, trips, statistics and employees.
